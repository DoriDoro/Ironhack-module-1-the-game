Introduction

You see pictures of known animals on the right side of the page. You will see the German name including the title of this animal and the English name too in little boxes.

The task is to learn how to write the German name including the title of the displayed animals during 10 seconds and remember it.

After 10 seconds you have to write the German name of the animal in the textfield below the German name box. For this task you have 30 seconds of time.

For the special characters in German you find them in the little box under the testfield where you write down your answer.

On the first page of the game you will find an easy example.

Good luck!
