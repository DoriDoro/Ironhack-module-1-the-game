Introduction

You will see pictures of known animals on the right side of the page. In little boxes you will see the German name including the title of this animal and the English name too.

The task is to learn how to write the German name including the title of the displayed animals during 10 seconds and remember it.

After 10 seconds you have to write the German name of the animal in the textfield below the German name box. For this task you have 15 seconds of time.

The special characters in German language you find them in the little box under the textfield where you write down your answer.


Good luck!
